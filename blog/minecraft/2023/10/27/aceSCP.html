aceSCP

the area where everyone was at basically

in writing this blog post, i hope to document stuff most things from my old minecraft server that i had hosted for my friends so that i don’t have to load up this huge world and its RAM-hungry mods every time i wanna look at something.

SCP: Lockdown and Parasites were the two most popular mods that were hosted on the server, though I think people eventually found parasites annoying (me too)… and there were a few who really liked Thaumcraft too so i don’t want to forget that one. i got around to calling this world “aceSCP” because well… i couldn’t remember if I even gave my server a name when I hosted this for my friends. however, i really needed a name to call the backup of this world. so… the one main thing i remember from this server was my friend Ace overseeing a big ass SCP facility and ta-da that’s where the name is inspired from.

when it came time to close the server due to dying interest, i archived the world just for keepsake and made a lil replaymod cinematic of me running around the world. it was a way to not only stress my GPU a lil to see how it could record videos, but also for me to make a visual archive of most of the structures made by my friends.

seeing everyone's build in the video is nice looking back at it. i think its sweet my friends and i came together to make something cool and fun to play around with during the downtime. thank you to whoever played on this server. thank you to my friends who stuck with me when i troubleshooted the fuck out of this server.

the pizza SCP

THIS PIZZA IS MAGIC. ITS EXISTENCE IS TOO BROKEN.
ONE BOX. INFINITE PIZZAS.

just did a google search and apparently this is SCP-458. what’s funny is that the SCP states that the SCP itself is a little ceasers box. typing this entry up, I was literally thinking about this being a Hot-N-Ready box without even knowing the SCP’s story lolol

but yeah you can imagine with a never-ending pizza box like this, all food items we previously had could be rendered useless unless you didn’t have the box. eating a slice replenished a good amount of hunger too.
